Join leaders of two longstanding community organizations who stepped into their roles in chaotic times. Both joined their organizations fairly recently, at moments when their predecessors had planned for significant growth but not the infrastructure to support it. Staff were underpaid, key relationships were missing, long-term financial planning was lacking. 
In this session you will hear the story of each presenter, followed by a lot of Q&A time. Bring your stories and your questions!
