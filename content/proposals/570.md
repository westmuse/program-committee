Philanthropy is shifting, and museums must translate national giving trends into locally relevant fundraising strategies. This session pairs insights from the _2025_ _Giving USA: The Annual Report on Philanthropy_ with real-world stories from across the sector. Participants will explore how historical and current giving data shapes donor behavior in the arts, culture, and humanities sector. The presenter will share how national trends informed strategic decisions, messaging, and donor engagement efforts in museums.
