What are the powerful practices we should use to ensure an engaging and quality visitor experience? Using workshop techniques, participants in this session will work in small groups to inventory and catalog the best practices they use or have observed in developing walks, talks and tours. What is the best way to begin a program? What are good practices to carry the program? How do we end our program that leaves the visitor with wanting more?
