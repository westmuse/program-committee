This panel examines the acquisition process for academic art museums with complex relationships to external. Representatives from both collections and curatorial departments will share strategies that succeeded - or failed - in managing complicated accessions while navigating institutional protocols and external relationships. From the Utah Museum of Fine Arts, the acquisition of _LOVE_ _(Red Outside Blue Inside)_ will be used as a case study, alongside the Jordan Schnitzer Museum of Art’s case study that examines donor/external relations.
