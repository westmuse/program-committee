At the Natural History Museum of Utah, _A Climate of Hope (ACoH) _offers a path to rational hope. This exhibit shows how climate change affects Utah’s people and places, while also helping visitors envision a healthy, prosperous, low-carbon future. In this session, exhibit developers and learning researchers share stories from _ACoH_, from prototyping to research-based iteration that continues today. Audience members participate in activities to collectively imagine new ways of supporting visitor learning towards constructive hope amid climate change.
