Bring your lessons-learned, best ideas, and biggest fears around construction, expansion and renovation projects. Hear from designers, engineers—and each other! Become empowered to start the design process with deeper knowledge and clearer expectations. 

The panel will advocate for robust pre-design phases, as you define your project, establish your values and determine design criteria most important to you and your team. Leverage terms like _flexibility, resiliency_ and _sustainability_ to set up a successful project.
