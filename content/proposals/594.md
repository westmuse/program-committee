The Natural History Museum of Utah’s _STEM Rooted in Culture_ (SRIC) educator workshop series brings together community experts and museum educators. Together we help educators broaden understanding and diversify teaching practices in the K-12 social studies and STEM classroom.

Participants in this half-day workshop will experience past workshop highlights first-hand. NHMU and our partners will share our efforts to elevate Indigenous knowledge, cultivate sustained and meaningful community partnerships, and increase equity and access for students.
